# CS320 Team 4 - Volunteer App

## About The Project
Created for CS 320 at University of Massachusetts Amherst, _**{Name}**_ is a platform that connects volunteers to local opportunities in just a few steps. 

Volunteers can browse and filter available events, apply to opportunities, and track their past and upcoming commitments, all while accumulating an easily accessible total volunteer hour count. Organizations can post events, manage applicants, and accept or deny volunteers, giving them full visibility into the volunteers signing up for their events.

The goal of this project is to reduce the frustration that both sides of volunteering may face. Students and volunteers struggle to discover local opportunities, while organizations, despite actively seeking help, are limited in their reach and ability to recruit. _**{Name}**_ bridges this gap by giving both volunteers and organizations a centralized, easy-to-use space to find each other. 

This application was created by Lior Carmeli, Luke Santos, Euba Tafese, Han Dang, Adhya Putta, Jashika Kaur, Emily Lu, Michael Kimmer, and Liyana Eshaal, overseen by Sarang Kale.
